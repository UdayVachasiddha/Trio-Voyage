Our story — travel with care

From a small group of destination-curious travellers to a boutique tour operator — we craft journeys rooted in local expertise and thoughtful planning.

Our beginning

A simple idea: better travel, less fuss

TrioVoyage started when three friends realised travellers wanted curated, reliable small-group experiences with local experts. Over the years we’ve built trusted partnerships across hotels, transport and local guides to deliver trips that feel effortless and memorable.

Milestones

  • 2018
    Founded
    TrioVoyage began as a weekend escape organiser in the city. Small groups, local guides.
  • 2019
    First international trips
    Launched curated trips to nearby countries and built partner networks abroad.
  • 2021
    Trusted guides program
    Built an onboarding & training program for local guides to ensure consistent guest experiences.
  • 2024
    Expanded services
    Added visas, flights and bespoke group bookings — now serving travellers across regions.

Our mission

We design journeys that connect people with places and locals. Safety, experience and service are our top priorities — from the hotels we choose to the guides we train.

Values

  • Quality — handpicked partners and vetted hotels.
  • Local knowledge — expert guides & local connections.
  • Care — supportive service from enquiry to return.
